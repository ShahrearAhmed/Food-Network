Our Most Popular Recipies

Eating healthy doesn't have to be difficult or boring. To prove it, our team of talented registered dietitian nutritionists at Henry Ford Health System routinely develops recipes that are full of nutritional value and great taste. They write instructions that are clear and easy to follow, and focus on ingredients that are not too expensive or hard to find. Then, they test them, tweak them and test them again until they get a final recipe that's just right. They meticulously calculate the nutritional information, so that if you are watching your sodium intake or counting calories, you'll have all the details you need when you prepare one of these recipes.
